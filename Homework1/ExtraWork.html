Beagle
Weight: 10-11 kg
Coat: Short haired,hard coat of medium length
Color: Many colors

The beagle is a breed of small hound that is similar in appearance to the much larger foxhound. The beagle is a scent hound, developed primarly for hunting hare (beagling). With a great sense of smell and a superior tracking instinct, the beagle is employed as detection dog for prohibited agricultural imports and foodstuffs in quarantine around the world. The beagle is intelligent but single-minded. It is a popular pet due to its font-size, good temper, and lack of inherited health problems.
